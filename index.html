About Me

Hey there! I’m Anish Gurung, an SEO specialist with a passion for making websites rank, perform, and convert. With two years of experience in both on-page and off-page SEO, I’ve had the pleasure of working with various clients to enhance their online presence and boost their business profiles.

How It All Started

Like most digital marketing enthusiasts, my journey into SEO started with curiosity. I was fascinated by how search engines work, why some websites rank higher than others, and what magic happens behind the scenes. The more I learned, the more I realized SEO isn’t just about keywords and backlinks—it’s about strategy, user experience, and staying ahead of Google’s ever-changing algorithms.

My SEO Philosophy

SEO isn’t just about rankings—it’s about visibility, credibility, and user trust. I focus on sustainable SEO practices that not only help businesses climb the SERPs but also maintain their position long-term. Quick wins are great, but real success comes from consistency and adaptability.
